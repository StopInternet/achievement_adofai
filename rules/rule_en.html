Adofai JP Unofficial


Thank you for joining the Adofai JP Unofficial Discord Server!
Please follow the rules below in this server.
If you violate the rules, the moderators will take the appropriate actions, which will result in a permanent ban at worst.
Please read the rules carefully.


Rules
1. Do not send messages/converse about the following against others:
・Racism (includes abbreviations and euphemisms)
・Slanders, offense
・Impersonating
・NSFW content (Sexual, violent, and cruel content)
・Personal information (such as address, phone number) (includes information about the sender) and messages that violate privacy
・Posts that may violate the laws and moral


2. Do not send excessive mentions to others.
Sending reactions etc. does not count.


3. Vandalism, such as spamming in channels may result in a permanent ban.


How to join the server
1. Verify your mail address.
2. Go to #welcome, and press the button sended by Role Overseer.
3. (Optional) If you are streaming or if you want to get notifications by streamers,
go to #streamers and press the buttons there.
4. Welcome to the Adofai JP Unofficial Discord Server!
